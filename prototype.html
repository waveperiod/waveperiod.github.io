What is GAS Training?

GAS (General Adaptation Syndrome) is a training principle developed by scientist Hans Selye. It describes how your body responds to stress in three phases:

  • Shock Phase (Weeks 1-3): Your body encounters new stress and adapts
  • Adaptation Phase (Weeks 5-7): Your body gets stronger to handle the stress
  • Peak Phase (Weeks 9-11): You reach your maximum performance

Wave combines GAS with your menstrual cycle to optimize when you push hard and when you recover. This means better results without burnout.

The Wave Advantage: Unlike generic plans, we adjust intensity based on your hormonal phases. When estrogen is high (follicular/ovulation), we push harder. When progesterone dominates (luteal/menstrual), we focus on recovery.
